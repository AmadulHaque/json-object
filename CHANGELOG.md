## [Unreleased]

### Added
- Core `JsonObject` class with schema and casting support.
- `JsonCaster` for Eloquent integration.
- `make:json` Artisan command.
- `HasAccessors` trait for get/set operations.
- `HasValidation` trait and `ValidatesJson` contract.
- `TracksDirtyAttributes` trait.
- `HasLogging` trait.
- `Macroable` support for extensibility.
- Comprehensive unit and feature tests.
